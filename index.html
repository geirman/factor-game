Rules

fac•tor /fakter/

1. a number or quantity that when multiplied with another produces a given number or expression.

  • Play alternates between players
  • Each player chooses a number. To be a valid choice, that number must not have previously been played and have available (unplayed) factors.
  • Player making the selection receives the number of points equal to their selection, but gives away the total points of all available (unplayed) factors of the chosen number.
  • Player with the most points at the end, wins!

Factors Game 3.0
